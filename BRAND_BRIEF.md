# Apex Ride — Brand & Logo Brief

## Logo design paragraph (copy this)

Apex Ride is a premium private-hire and taxi service based in Manchester, UK, built around the promise of a fixed, transparent fare with no surge pricing. The name "Apex" evokes the racing line through a corner — the fastest, most precise path — so the brand should feel sharp, confident, and performance-driven, yet still refined and trustworthy enough for executive and airport travel. The logo pairs a short "APEX" wordmark (set in the geometric, slightly futuristic Space Grotesk typeface) with an icon that suggests motion and precision: an upward apex/chevron, a peak, or an abstract racing line bending around a corner — ideally hinting at a road or route. The palette is a bold signature red-orange (#F23A2F) and an energetic amber-gold (#FFB300) against a warm near-black (#17120E) or warm cream (#F6F2EC) — never pure white. Critically, the mark must read cleanly in both light and dark modes, so it should be delivered on a transparent background with full-color, single-color, and reversed (white) variants. The overall feel: modern, energetic, premium, and unmistakably about getting there fast and on a clear, honest fare.

## Quick reference

- **Name:** Apex Ride (wordmark: APEX)
- **Sector:** Premium private-hire / taxi, Manchester UK
- **Personality:** Sharp, fast, precise, premium, trustworthy
- **Mark ideas:** upward apex/chevron, peak, abstract racing line bending around a corner, route/road hint

### Colors
- Primary red-orange: **#F23A2F**
- Amber-gold: **#FFB300**
- Ink / dark bg: **#17120E**
- Warm cream / light bg: **#F6F2EC**
- Dark surface: **#13100E**

### Type
- Display / wordmark: **Space Grotesk**
- Body: **Manrope**
- Arabic (RTL support): **Cairo** (display) + **Tajawal** (body)

### Must-haves
- Works on BOTH light (cream) and dark (near-black) backgrounds
- Deliver on transparent background
- Provide full-color, single-color (mono), and reversed (white) versions
- Geometric style to match Space Grotesk

## Logo deliverables to request
1. Primary horizontal lockup — icon + "APEX" / "Apex Ride" wordmark
2. Stacked / vertical lockup — icon above wordmark
3. Icon-only mark — square, for app icon / favicon / avatar (must read at 32px)
4. Monochrome + reversed (white-on-dark) versions
5. Clean SVG geometry
